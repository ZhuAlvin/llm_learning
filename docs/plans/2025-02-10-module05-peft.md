**Step 1: Add LoRA theory**

Explain LoRA: freeze pre-trained weights, add trainable low-rank decomposition matrices. Show the formulation:

$$W' = W + BA$$

where $W \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times k}$, $B \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times r}$, $A \in \mathbb{R}^{r \times k}$, and $r \ll \min(d,k)$

**Step 2: Implement LoRA from scratch**
